Why by application

Most matrimonial apps let anyone in. That's the problem.

Ikhlaas is different by design. Every applicant is screened before they can join — for genuine intent to marry, consistent practice, and sincerity of deen. It's why members here are serious in a way they aren't anywhere else.

Intent, not curiosity

You apply by declaring a real intention to marry. Browsers and time-wasters don't make it past the door.

Practice, honestly asked

We ask about salah, deen, and the way you live — because compatibility of practice is what actually lasts.

Reviewed, not automated away

Every application is reviewed by our team. Fewer than four in ten are accepted.

Verified, really

A selfie liveness check confirms every member is a real person who is who they say they are. No catfish, no fakes.

Built the right way

Every detail respects the way marriage should begin.

Your guardian, involved

Invite your Wali through a private portal. Dignified, simple, the way it should be.

Your photos, your terms

Blurred by default. Revealed only when you choose. Modesty is the setting, not an afterthought.

Guarded conversations

No endless chatting. Conversations are limited, purposeful, and made to lead somewhere real — a nikah, inshaAllah.

A space to leave

The goal is that you meet someone and go. We measure our success by the marriages that begin here.
